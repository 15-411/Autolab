Contact Us

  • Questions about a grade on an assignment?

    Contact the individual who graded you, and also your instructor. You can find out who graded a particular problem by clicking on that score while viewing your handin history.

  • Questions about error messages you are receiving?

    If you are receiving an error message from Autolab, either as a list of why you cannot do something, or a generic "Donkey Kong" page, please contact the Autolab Development team at autolab-dev@andrew.cmu.edu, and we will get back to you.

  • Feature Requests?

    Send them to the autolab development team at autolab-dev@andrew.cmu.edu.

  • Want to work for us?

    We are currently seeking several talented and motivated individuals to help out on the development of both the frontend and the backend of Autolab, please contact the development team at autolab-dev@andrew.cmu.edu if you are interested.
